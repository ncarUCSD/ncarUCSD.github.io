The Problem

Face masks and PPE have run out for healthcare workers and they are pleading on social media for more.

How Mask Share UK works

We are a project that connects people with PPE to NHS healthcare workers in need

Mask and PPE donors fill in this form.

Mask and PPE donors fill in this donation form with how much of our accepted masks and PPE they can provide (see what PPE we allow here).

NHS healthcare workers fill in this form

Healthcare workers fill in this receiving form and send us a verification email with their @nhs.net email and the subject line "VERIFICATION" to team@maskshare.co.uk.

The postal service pick up the PPE and deliver

The donor selects a postal service to send the PPE. We recommend Parcel Hero that can arrange a contactless pickup.

What masks we do/don't accept

Note: Mask Share UK is not responsible for the quality, delivery or contamination of any of the masks or PPE (Read our Terms and Conditions for more). Please double check as a donor on the box what type of masks and PPE you have.

✅ N95, P95, R95 or KN95 masks

Please check on the box and packaging that you actually have N95, P95, R95 or KN95 masks. These are respirator masks typically characterised by having a small respirator box within them.

✅ FFP3 Respirator masks

Please check on the box and packaging that you actually have FFP3 masks. These are respirator masks typically characterised by having a small respirator box within them.

✅ Surgical Masks

Please check on the box and packaging that you actually have surgical masks.

✅ Dust Masks

Please check on the box and packaging that you actually have dust masks. These may look similar to N95 masks, but do not contain a respiratory filter.

❌ Homemade masks

We currently do not accept homemade masks, but are looking to expand to this. We do accept 3D printed visors and goggles though!

What other PPE we accept

Note: Mask Share UK is not responsible for the quality, delivery or contamination of any of the PPE (Read our Terms and Conditions for more). Please double check as a donor on the box what type of PPE you have.

✅ Disposable gloves

Please check on the box and packaging that you actually have fluid resistant diposable gloves.

✅ Disposable fluid-resistant gowns

Please check on the box and packaging that you actually have disposable fluid-resistant gowns.

✅ Medical goggles

Please check on the box and packaging that you actually have medical goggles.

✅ Medical visors

Please check on the box and packaging that you actually have medical visors.

✅ 3D-printed visors and goggles

We allow donations of 3D-printed visors and goggles for healthcare workers that are willing to accept these.

Answers to some of our most common questions

Mask Share UK is a peer-to-peer platform to help people with spare PPE send them directly to healthcare workers on the front lines who need them now. Many hospitals are limiting the number of PPE doctors and nurses can use due to limited quantities on hand and saving up for the anticipated peak, and many are forced to go without due to lack of supply. For example, Dr Samantha Batt-Rawden, the president of the Doctors’ Association UK (DAUK) states “Lack of personal protective equipment continues to be a critical issue. It is heartbreaking to hear that some staff have been told to simply ‘hold their breath’ due to lack of PPE. We find this concerning and want to do our best to contribute to the NHS during this difficult period."
